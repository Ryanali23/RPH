1521 to 1898

Spanish Era

The Spanish colonization era brought down a myriad of catastrophes upon the Philippines’ Constitutional state. As stated by Rizal, the Filipinos used to be more independent—brimming with culture, just before the Spaniards had colonized us. At this era, the constitution of the Philippines alone could arguably have been considered to be almost non-existent. This was due to the fact that a lot of the Spanish authorities had taken control of the dominance over most of the constitutional areas of the Philippines.

Most Notable Things that happened during pre Colonial Era:

  • The evangelization of the Filipinos
  • The infamous Cavite Mutiny
  • The execution of the Gomburza
  • Finally, the birth of the GomBurZa - Filipino's movement for freedom and independence
